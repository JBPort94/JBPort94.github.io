Not So Social

Not So Social is a comedy podcast that I produce with two other co-hosts. It is meant for entertainment value. We spend about an hour discussing several topics on media, life, and everything in between. Launching late March! Find us wherever you get your podcasts.

SCP Foundation Station

SCP Foundation Station is a comedic/informative podcast where my two co-hosts and I dive deep into the fictional world of SCPs. We help digest the scattered information into easy to listen to episodes! We cover anything from the background of the SCP Foundation to the mysterious coffee machines that will take any order. Catch us on launch day, March 30th!
